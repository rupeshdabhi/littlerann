How to reach Eco Camp?
By Air:
The nearest Air ports are Ahmadabad about 190 k.m., Rajkot 190 k.m. and Bhuj is about 200 k.m.

By Train:
Dhrangadhra is 45 k.m. and all kutch express train stopage situated around 18 k.m from the Sanctuary and 130 k.m. from Ahmadabad railway station. The place is ;connected to all the major cities in the region.

By Road:
Dhrangadhra is well connected with other important places in the Govt.State by the State Transport buses, as well as luxury coaches operated by private companies.

Dhrangadhra town is situated on the very Ahmadabad – Kutch National High way Road.
All the Buses and Trains from Ahmadabad – Bhuj – Kutch – Express stop at Dhrangdhra.
